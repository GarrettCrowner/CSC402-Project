## Colors
1. University Classic
- classic colors, used for athletics and around campus
- Classic Purple (#512d6d) & Classic Gold (#ffc72c)

2. Brand Primary 
- updated versions of purple and gold for rebrand
- Elemental Purple (#382140) & Elemental Gold (#ffe800)

3. Brand Secondary
- complement the brand primary
- Accent Purple (#6e3061) & Accent Gold (#d4a129)

## Digital Color Use
- contrast between colors is high enough to pass WGAC 2.0 AA accessiblity standards
- any color combos with AA desigination need to be a font size of 1.15 em or higher to pass ADA
- digital color use guide is [here](https://www.wcupa.edu/brand/colors.aspx)
    - color combos with dotted lines can't be used

## Fonts
- display primary print/digital -> phonk contrast
- display secondary print/digital -> phonk regular
- body copy digital -> myriad pro, italic, bold, bold italic 
- digital use: myriad pro is used for digital body copy, can't be used as a display font
    - phonk contrast and phonk regular may be used as display fonts for digital application
